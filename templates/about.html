About ThreatLens

ThreatLens is an intelligent cyber analysis platform designed to simplify and strengthen malware detection and threat analysis.

Our system leverages modern data-driven techniques to analyze executable files, extract meaningful features, and provide reliable insights for security researchers, students, and professionals.

The goal of ThreatLens is to bridge the gap between complex cybersecurity concepts and practical, user-friendly analysis tools.

We believe in clarity, accuracy, and accessibility in cyber intelligence.
